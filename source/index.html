Protect yourself against cyber security fraud
Fraudsters have various approaches to trick you into making an unwanted payment, compromising your personal and/ or financial information as well as lead you to sites that could potentially be malware.
Below are top tips to help you wise up to fraud
Always question uninvited approaches via emails, SMS or Voice Call

Do not share your personal information unless required and you know the purpose behind sharing it
Please create unique passwords with information only you would know (e.g. Do not use your or family members' birthdays, anniversary dates, etc)
